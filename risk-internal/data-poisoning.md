# Data poisoning

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/data-poisoning`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-established">established</span>

Adversarial manipulation of training or fine-tuning data to introduce backdoors, bias, or systematic errors into the model. Includes both broad-spectrum poisoning (degrading overall performance) and targeted poisoning (creating exploitable triggers or specific failure modes).

**Also known as:** Training data poisoning · Backdoor attacks

**Applies to:** ALL  
**Lifecycle stages:** Pre Processing, In Processing
