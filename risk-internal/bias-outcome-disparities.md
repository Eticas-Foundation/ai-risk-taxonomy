# Outcome disparities

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/bias-outcome-disparities`

Risks related to unequal treatment, allocation, or performance outcomes across demographic and protected groups. These are observable differences in what the system does to or for different groups.

## Subcategories

- [Differential performance across populations](performance-equity.md)
- [Disparate impact on protected groups](disparate-impact-protected-groups.md)
