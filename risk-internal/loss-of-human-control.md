# Loss of meaningful human control

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/loss-of-human-control`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

AI systems operating at speeds or complexity levels that make meaningful human oversight impractical, including inability to understand, interrupt, or reverse autonomous decisions.

> **This subcategory is emerging.** It has not yet been validated through established assessment methods.

**Applies to:** ALL  
**Lifecycle stages:** Post Processing
