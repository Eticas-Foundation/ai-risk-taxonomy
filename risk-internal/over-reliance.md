# Over-reliance on AI

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/over-reliance`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

Systemic dependence on AI systems that leaves individuals or organisations unable to function when the system is unavailable or produces incorrect outputs.

> **This subcategory is emerging.** It has not yet been validated through established assessment methods.
