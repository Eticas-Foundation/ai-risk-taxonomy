# Representational harm

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/bias-representational-harm`

Risks related to how the system represents groups in its outputs — through stereotypes, toxic content, skewed representation, or homogenization. These are harms in the form of meaning and content rather than allocation of resources or opportunities.

## Subcategories

- [Geographic, cultural, or language skew](geographic-cultural-language-skew.md)
- [Stereotyping and demeaning content](stereotyping-demeaning-content.md)
- [Harmful content and toxicity](harmful-content-toxicity.md)
- [Sentiment and quality disparity across groups](sentiment-fairness.md)
- [Homogenization of output across groups](homogenization-output-across-groups.md)
