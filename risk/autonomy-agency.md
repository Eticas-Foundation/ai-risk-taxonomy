# Autonomy & Agency

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/autonomy-agency`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

The risk that an AI system undermines human agency or operates with insufficient human control. This includes over-reliance on AI outputs, automation bias, deskilling, and — for agentic AI systems — failure modes arising from multi-step autonomous actions, tool use, or coordination with other AI agents.

> **This category is emerging.** Assessment methods are still being developed. Definitions and subcategories may evolve.

**Also known as:** Autonomy & Human Agency · Agentic Risks · Human-AI Interaction

**Applies to:** ALL  
**Lifecycle stages:** Post Processing
