# Multi-step autonomous actions

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/multi-step-actions`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

AI systems that plan and execute sequences of actions, where errors in early steps cascade unpredictably through later steps.

> **This subcategory is emerging.** It has not yet been validated through established assessment methods.

**Applies to:** LLM
