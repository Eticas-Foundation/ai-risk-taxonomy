# Incident Reporting & Redress

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/incident-reporting-redress`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

The risk that when AI-driven decisions cause harm, there are no effective mechanisms to report incidents, escalate concerns, challenge decisions, or seek remedy. This includes absence of appeal mechanisms, unclear remediation procedures, and failure to communicate with affected parties.

> **This category is emerging.** Assessment methods are still being developed. Definitions and subcategories may evolve.

**Also known as:** Responsibility & Redress

**Applies to:** ALL  
**Lifecycle stages:** Post Processing
