# Workflow compatibility

`https://taxonomy.eticas.ai/risk/workflow-compatibility`

**Maturity:** <span class="badge badge-emerging">emerging</span>

The system's outputs, alerts, or interfaces do not fit existing human workflows, leading to alert fatigue, workarounds, or abandonment. Includes inadequate end-user involvement in design and testing, and poor context transferability from development to deployment environment.

> **This subcategory is emerging.** It has not yet been validated through established assessment methods.

**Applies to:** ALL  
**Lifecycle stages:** Post Processing
